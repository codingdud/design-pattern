# Keep It Simple Stupide
The idea is straightforward: keep your code simple and avoid unnecessary complexity.
- Is easy to read and understand.
- Minimizes the number of moving parts (functions, classes, methods).
- Avoids overly clever solutions that might confuse future developers.
The goal is not sacrificing functionality but designing the simplest solution possible without compromising performance or usability.

- scalability
